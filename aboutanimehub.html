AnimeHub was created to provide anime fans with a simple, fast, and organized platform to stream
and discover their favorite anime series and movies.
The app was developed to solve common problems such as slow streaming, difficult navigation,
and limited genre filtering found in other platforms.

AnimeHub aims to:
🎬 Provide high-quality anime streaming
📂 Offer well-organized categories
🔍 Make searching easy and fast
❤️ Allow users to create watchlists
🔔 Notify users of new episodes

The goal of AnimeHub is to become a centralized hub for anime lovers,
combining accessibility, modern design, and smooth performance in one application.
